About K-Eleventh Global

Delivering global standard automotive services since 1998

OUR STORY

From Local Workshop to Global Network

K-Eleventh Global began as a small auto repair shop in Lagos with a vision to bring world-class automotive service to Africa.

Founded by Chief Osakwe Oredia in 1998, we've grown from a single-bay workshop to an international network of service centers across West Africa, Europe, and North America.

Our name reflects our founder's commitment to the 11 key principles of automotive excellence that guide every aspect of our business.
